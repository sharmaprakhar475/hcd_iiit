    • About Us

                  

    We at HCD@IIITD, focus on finding innovative solutions to challenges one faces every time a new technology is adopted by the masses. We offer an interdisciplinary undergraduate program in Computer Science and Design (B.Tech), that aims to produce the next-generation engineers having robust skills related to Computer Animation, Game Design, Digital Media Design and Editing, VR/AR, to name a few. Our research students (PhDs), on the other hand, push the boundaries of Human-Machine Interaction, Design and the pertinent underlying challenges relevant to physics, mathematics, human psychology, vision, to only count a few
